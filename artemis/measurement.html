Download the Artemis Impact app, and Effect Change

One of the most accessible, convenient keys to starting your organization’s journey towards a data-driven impact-driven future is through downloading our Artemis Impact app.

With our web/mobile app your team will be able to achieve:

01

Convenient Access

The Artemis web/mobile Impact app will allow convenient access to your team whether they’re in the office, in the field, or anywhere in between. Your work is challenging, and your access needs to be right there where you are, and right at your fingertips!

02

Convenient Access

Your team’s specific Theory of Change impact will be driven by hard numbers and the Artemis Impact app will allow your team to collect, collate, and effectively communicate your data transparently.

This data will clearly reflect

A record of actual work hours/time expended on any/every task

An output-based record collected through focused surveys

A comprehensive overview of both time expenditures and the resulting actual output

03

Clearly Define Your ToC Terms

From Input to Output to Impact, it’s vital for your team to both understand and then be able to clearly articulate the specific parameters which your efforts will effect on your social and economic footprint. Your ToC Terms should clearly outline, define, and then drill down into a results-driven chain that’s both effective and transparent.

Easy Acces for All

Easy Access for All Whether your team is corporate, NGO, private enterprise or any size/shape in-between, once downloaded every team member will be able to conveniently access and share all vital, real-time data.
