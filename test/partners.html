Technology partner
IBS is a key technology partner of Russian business and government leaders. The company is transforming its team's knowledge and understanding of the modern economy into new solutions that help increase competitiveness and achieve better results in the digital age.

IBS solves complex tasks in the field of strategic development of the organization's it resources and improving the efficiency of processes, as well as provides services in the field of creating management systems, implementing business applications, designing and building it infrastructure, data management and Analytics, system integration, software development and unique custom solutions, information security and outsourcing.

IBS builds long-term partnerships with its clients, such as Aeroflot, Gazprom, Gazprombank, Rosatom, Rosneft, Sberbank, SIBUR, Transneft, X5, the Bank of Russia, the Russian Ministry of Finance, Rosavtodor, the Federal Treasury, the Federal tax service, the social insurance Fund, and many others, based on outstanding engineering experience, innovations, and deep industry expertise. IBS clients are most of the leading Russian companies*.
