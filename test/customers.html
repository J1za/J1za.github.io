Valenta Pharm is a Russian innovative pharmaceutical company established in 1997 and focused on development and production of new original medicines. Valenta Pharm is engaged in development, production, and market launch of prescription and OTC medicines for such therapeutic areas as immunology, virology and antibacterial therapy, psychoneurology, gastroenterology, urology, etc. The company's medicines portfolio comprises well-known brands as Ingavirin®, Grammidin®, Trimedat, Teraligen, Phenazepam, Pantocalcin®, Aminazin, etc. www.valentapharm.com
AKRIKHIN is one of the leading Russian pharmaceutical companies that produces effective, affordable and high quality medicines in the most popular therapeutic areas in Russia. The company is in the top 5 leading local pharmaceutical producers by sales on the Russian market*.

AKRIKHIN celebrated its 80th anniversary in 2016. The company is actively developing, placing an emphasis on modernization and expanding production, as well as embracing new technologies. As a result, the company’s performance grows every year, in terms of both production output and sales volume. AKRIKHIN is also working on ambitious plans for the future.

www.akrikhin.com
