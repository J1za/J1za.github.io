About

Equilibrium LLC is a consulting company that provides services for the implementation, development and support of Track&Trace solutions.

We help build business processes at all levels and implement information systems for implementing projects for serialization and full tracking of product movement, including integration with contractors, contract manufacturers and 3PL, based on industry standards, such as GS1.

Our team has long been working in the market of solutions for SAP and other systems for business process automation. Due to the tightening of legislation and regulatory requirements in such industries as pharmaceuticals, tobacco, and consumer goods, we have combined the efforts of experienced professionals and created the company equilibrium.

Today – it is a dynamically growing company with a 100% focus on the Track&Trace business. Now we have more than 20 specialists who have experience in implementing labeling projects around the world. We also constantly involve a network of our external partners and project resources.
