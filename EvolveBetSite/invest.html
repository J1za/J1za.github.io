Cost Per Action (CPA)

Is a payment model for online advertising, in which only certain user actions on the advertiser's website are paid. Key performance indicators

KPI or key performance indicators is a numerical expression of performance results, on the basis of which it becomes possible to visualize and analyze indicators of achieving the set goals. It is these data that serve to assess the effectiveness of work, and also allow you to optimize it over time.

Our company works with advanced software that allows us to make the most accurate KPI analysis. The system continuously collects information, which is studied by our specialists and on the basis of which we make decisions to improve the workflow.

Evolve has information on how to quickly and efficiently change KPIs in a positive way. In our activities, we take into account the classic and optimal indicators for the gaming industry and strive to comply with them. Below are the “correct” KPIs that we are focused on.

Financial KPI
GGR - the difference between the total amount of bets and the winnings of the players.
Example: $ 5000 Bets - $ 2500 Winnings = $ 2500 GGR.
NGR - casino profit after mandatory payments.
Example: GGR $ 2500 - taxes $ 680 - client bonuses $ 500 = $ 1320.
Custom KPI
Conversion rate - the percentage of new customers out of total ad views.
Example: 63 new signups / 1200 views * 100% = 5.25% conversion rate.
LTV - the total income of the player from the moment of his registration.
Example: APRU $ 20 * average activity time 18 months = LTV $ 360.
Player churn rate is the ratio of the number of users who stopped playing to the number of active customers. The indicator for the month is analyzed.
Example: Ainactive users / active users * 100% = churn rate 50%
Mixed KPI
Acquisition costs - the cost of attracting one player.
Example: FB advertising $ 100 + INSTAGRAM advertising $ 200/10 new users = $ 30 acquisition cost
ARPU - average income from a player.
Example: casino income per month $ 10,500 / number of players 525 = ARPU $ 20
