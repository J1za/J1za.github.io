Partner
program
The referral program is your opportunity to earn money for your first investment or to secure constant passive income for yourself without investing personal funds in EvolveBet.

We appreciate your work and we reward the members of the referral program on an ongoing basis.

Thus, you will receive a bonus from each deposit of your referrals for the entire life of the EvolveBet platform. We also take into account the number of active partners you have invited and your investment activity when calculating partner remuneration. The more effort and money you invest in the platform, the higher your status and, accordingly, the amount of income.

We are ready to pay a percentage of all deposits of his referrals to each partner who promotes and popularizes our platform.

The funds will be automatically credited to your account after the investment is made by the partner he invited. The company has developed a three-level referral program for you. This means that you will receive a percentage not only from the investments of the users you invited, but also from the deposits of their referrals, as well as partners invited by your friends' friends. Thus, if you assemble an active team, then the amount of your earnings will constantly increase.

Each partner can earn unlimited amounts.

This option will allow you not only to test EvolveBet and make sure the payments are timely, but also to make the referral program a permanent additional or even the main way of income. The company, for its part, also does its best to increase your earnings by providing promotional materials, making special offers, and by other methods. We always know how to help our partners and reward your efforts generously!

How does the referral program work?
registration
In order to make money on our referral program, you need to be a user of our platform. Register a personal account, providing us with the necessary data, and from that moment all the EvolveBet options become available to you.
presentation
Tell your friends about the benefits of cooperation with our company. Share your personal experience on our platform and offer to sign up. You can use our promotional materials for a more effective company presentation.
an invitation
Provide a potential referral with a registration link by copying it from your personal account. If necessary, help the new user create an account, place a deposit, and also understand the platform options so that he can then work on his own.
reward
Receive rewards for all investments of your partners up to the third level. Funds will be credited to your account automatically after every partner places a deposit. The amount of your payments directly depends on your status.
create new account
In order for you to receive a reward from the investments of your attracted partners, we need to «secure» them to you. Especially for this, a unique referral link is generated in your personal account, which you need to provide to a potential user for registration. It is this simple action that allows us to identify a new partner as your referral and to add rewards to your EvolveBet account. Especially for your convenience, we have posted step by step instructions below.
referral program statuses
When calculating remuneration for the referral program, we take into account not only the amount of the deposit and the level of the referral, but also the general activity of the partner on our platform. We always encourage your initiative. Therefore, EvolveBet users who work really hard on our platform receive increased referral credits. We present to your attention three statuses of our affiliate program.
Status: Jack
4% - 1% - 0.5%
This is the basic status of the Evolve affiliate program, which is available to our users immediately after completing the registration procedure on the platform. You may not even pre-place a deposit, but already invite new partners for cooperation and receive a reward. The size of your bonus for the referral program in Jack status will be 4% -1% -0.5%.
Status: King
6% - 1.5% - 1%
This status is assigned after you have placed deposits totaling $ 5,000 or have invited 25 active partners, each of whom has already made a contribution of any amount. Once any of the above goals are met, you will automatically receive King status. You are now eligible for an increased remuneration of 6% -1.5% -1% on an ongoing basis.
Status: Joker
8% - 2% - 1.5%
Upon obtaining this status, you are entitled to the highest possible reward under the Evolve referral program. To do this, you need to make an investment for a total of $ 20,000 or invite 100 referrals, each of whom will place at least one deposit. Your reward in Jocker status will be 8% -2% -1.5%.
How can I track my progress?

To make it easier for you to track your status, as well as achieve goals and set new ones, we have created a convenient information office for referrals. Here you can find all the data related to your activities within the referral program. Analysis of this information will allow you to optimize your activities and get the maximum benefit from cooperation with us.

In the information office, you can track the number of partners and active investors you have registered. In addition, you will receive detailed information about their deposits, including the amounts and currency of investment, as well as your rewards. Your current status and individual conditions for getting the next one are displayed here.

Promotional materials

Especially for partners who want to make money on our referral program, we have developed unique promotional materials that you can find on our website. They were created so that you can even more effectively represent our company to potential investors and receive the maximum number of registrations in the shortest possible time.

Use promo materials for personal presentations, as well as to advertise Evolve on social networks and forums, accompanied by your referral link. Attractive banners and structured information about our company will make it even easier and faster for you to get new people to your team and increase your income. We are happy to help you achieve our common goals.
