Health & Safety

In a post COVID-19 world, many organizations are faced with the challenge of getting all workers back into the workplace and working again. Employers have to create a safe and smooth return to work from home. Several issues need to be tackled to accommodate the needs of workers while maximizing employee safety, regulatory compliance, and overall profitability.

Code of Talent allows you to design health & safety training that ensures a seamless return to work by teaching employees everything they need to know about workplace safety in a post-COVID-19 world and helping them adopt the best practices that ensure it.

Onboarding

For any organization, it is critical to effectively select, onboard, coach, and develop their team members. Onboarding is particularly important as it is an employee’s first interaction with your organization. Since first impressions matter, you need to make the onboarding process seamless by tailoring it to the individual involved.

Code of Talent ensures this by allowing you to perform personalized onboardings, build performance with continuous onboarding, and create learning paths for each employee engagingly and cost-effectively.
